Schedule

Workshops start at 9 am each day and finish at 5 pm. The 3-day pre-conference workshops start on Monday, and the 2-day workshops start on Tuesday, with all workshops finishing on Wednesday. You cannot sign up for multiple workshops. The workshop ticket description will indicate the number of days.

The conference itself kicks off Wednesday evening with a welcome reception, followed by the opening keynote. Thursday and Friday are two full days of keynotes, talks, hands-on sessions, and a dedicated open space track.

The conference program committee is currently in the process of reviewing session proposals. Please check back later for the schedule.
